Question #1

Easy

Determine whether the sequence \( a_n = \frac{n}{n+1} \) converges, and if so, find its limit.

(A) Diverges
(B) \( 0 \)
(C) \( \frac{1}{2} \)
(D) \( 1 \)
(E) \( 2 \)

Question #2

Medium

Let \( f(x) = \begin{cases} x \sin\left(\frac{1}{x}\right) & \text{if } x \neq 0 \\ 0 & \text{if } x = 0 \end{cases} \). Is \( f \) continuous at \( x = 0 \)?

(A) Yes, and differentiable at \( x = 0 \)
(B) Yes, but not differentiable at \( x = 0 \)
(C) No, not continuous at \( x = 0 \)
(D) Yes, but unbounded near \( x = 0 \)
(E) No, because the limit does not exist

Question #3

Medium

Let \( f(x) = |x| \) on \([-1, 1]\). What is the value of the Riemann integral \( \int_{-1}^{1} f(x) \, dx \)?

(A) \( 0 \)
(B) \( 1 \)
(C) \( 2 \)
(D) \( -1 \)
(E) The integral does not exist

Question #4

Hard

In the metric space \( (\mathbb{R}, d) \) where \( d(x, y) = |x - y| \), which of the following sets is compact?

(A) \( (0, 1) \)
(B) \( [0, \infty) \)
(C) \( [0, 1] \)
(D) \( \mathbb{Z} \)
(E) \( \{ \frac{1}{n} : n \in \mathbb{N} \} \)

Question #5

Hard

In \( \mathbb{R} \) with the standard topology, which of the following sets is closed?

(A) \( (0, 1) \)
(B) \( \mathbb{Q} \)
(C) \( [0, 1] \cap \mathbb{Q} \)
(D) \( \{ \frac{1}{n} : n \in \mathbb{N} \} \)
(E) \( (-\infty, 0) \cup (0, \infty) \)

Question #6

Medium

Suppose \( f: \mathbb{R} \to \mathbb{R} \) is differentiable and \( |f'(x)| \leq 1 \) for all \( x \in \mathbb{R} \). If \( f(0) = 0 \), what is the maximum possible value of \( f(2) \)?

(A) \( 0 \)
(B) \( 1 \)
(C) \( 2 \)
(D) \( 4 \)
(E) No maximum exists
