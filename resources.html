Test-Taking Tips from the Internet

  • Practice time management - you have about 2.5 minutes per question
  • Bring earplugs
  • Use elimination strategies for multiple choice questions
  • Don't spend too much time on any single question
  • Review basic computational skills regularly
  • Focus on understanding concepts, not just memorizing formulas
